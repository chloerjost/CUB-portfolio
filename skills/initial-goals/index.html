Initial Goals

I would like to focus on sound editing and production. I have some experience with electronic and computer music production using Logic Pro X, but I would like to use this opportunity to focus on audio recording with a more pared-down and accessible program (Audacity).

I will begin with watching the Lynda tutorial “Learning Audacity” since I have never used the program before. This includes areas such as:
Adjusting volume automatically
Removing unwanted background noise
Applying compression
(2 hours)
In terms of production, I want to learn:
Recording production-quality audio (0.5 hours)
Creating tracks with variety (1 hour)
Sounds foley effects (1 hour)
Tricks to make separate pieces feel cohesive (2 hours)
Mastering an “album” (2 hours)
I would like to learn more about how to fully utilize effects such as:
Reverb
EQ (low pass filters, high pass filters, notch filters)
Compression
(2 hours)

Because a lot of this will begin to make sense through application, I plan to spend a majority of my time creating work and utilizing these skills within the program. I would like to create a series of at least 4 musical pieces that experiment with audio that I record and process within Audacity. I am interested in the idea of abstract storytelling through sound. I am sure that creating these pieces will require at least 10 hours.
